davisREED is a large contractor that has the power of any large firm but does their work with the values of a small company. We were brought on to help davisREED with re-imaging their brand as they attracted more high-end clientele.

davisREED is an established company that knows what they stand for and what their values are, we just helped put that down on paper for them in a concise manner.
Once we had the brand equity in place we helped to crispen their messaging and establish their point of differentiation within the industry.

We designed and programmed a new website with their new messaging that highlighted their values and their new work.
